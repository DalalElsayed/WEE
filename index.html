Marketing :

The strategic marketing process involves conducting research and setting goals and objectives that will increase the effectiveness and success of your overall marketing strategy.

This process is useful because it helps you to be more assertive in your marketing, as you will be able to ensure that you are targeting the right audience, entering the right markets, and using the right channels.

You can think of it this way: Strategic marketing is the butter you put on the bread, you get the bread as is but the butter enhances the flavor and makes the bread better. Strategic marketing ensures that your marketing campaigns are well-planned, effective and visible to the right people. .

Business Development :
